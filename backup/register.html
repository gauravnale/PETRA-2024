Registration Information:

Some more information about registration for students and non-students is as follows:

  • Student and non-student registration includes all conference materials, access to proceedings, coffee breaks, Welcome Reception and Gala Dinner with live entertainment.
  • Registered students and non-students do not need to buy social activity tickets as they are included with the registration fee.
  • For each accepted paper, there needs to be one regular registration made in the appropriate timeframe for the paper to be included in the ACM conference proceedings.
  • For papers where the only author is a student, then a regular full registration is expected.
  • A limited number of remote conference participants will be allowed to provide pre-recorded paper presentations, will have access to all sessions and receive all conference materials electronically.
