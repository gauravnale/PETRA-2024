Please send your proposals or any questions you have to the Workshop Co-chairs, Dr. Maher Abujelala (maher.abujelala@mavs.uta.edu), Dr. Ashwin Ramesh Babu (ashwin.rameshbabu@mavs.uta.edu) and Dr. Akilesh Rajavenkatanarayanan (akilesh.rajavenkatanarayanan@mavs.uta.edu).   3. List of Topics
  4. Goals
  5. List of Workshop Organizers (including: Names, Affiliations and Emails)
