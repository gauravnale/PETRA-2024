How to get to Corfu

By Airplane
Corfu is very easily reachable from Athens with daily flights (1 hour). The airline companies that cover this route are the Olympic Air and the Aegean Airlines among others. There are also direct charter from many European cities to Corfu. Corfu island’s airport, named Ioannis Kapodistrias, is 2 Km far from Corfu town and you can reach it by taxi, buses or private vehicles. There is a bus that connects the airport with the city and the stops are: Garitsa, Alexandras street, San Rocco square (in the centre of the city), the central bus station and the port. The cost is 1,50 € and you can buy the ticket directly on the bus.

By Ferry Boat
There are many companies both Greek and Italian that from Italy can take you to Corfù island by ferry boat (Minoan, Hellenic, Anek, Superfast, Blue Star, Ventouris...) with departure from Venezia, Ancona, Bari, Brindisi and arrival to Igoumenitsa. From Igoumenitsa you need to take a ferry boat to go to Corfu. From Kerkyra you can also reach the near small islands of Paxos (1 hour) and Diapondia islands with departure from Sidari, A. Stefanos or Corfu town.
