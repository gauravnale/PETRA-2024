Demo Papers

Petra 2023 is pleased to announce the inclusion of a Demo Session in its 2023 Program. The purpose of the Demo session is to provide you a great opportunity to present your original research work towards creating a commericially viable product and receive valuable reviews from Demo committee. To qualify in this category- Your paper needs to be unpulished and should contribute towards a commercial application. The conference program will allocate a separate session for Demo paper presentations as posters. Authors must use ACM Master Article templates and prepare their manuscript in single column format using Word or LaTeX for reviews.

Requirements:

The authors must submit the manuscript of Demo paper (about 2 pages long including refs.) and a video presentation.


Video Submission Guidelines:

  1. The video must be in .mp4 format and use h.264 encoding. Other formats are not accepted.
  2. Please add a subtitle to your video to make it more accessible.
  3. It is NOT possible to process additional files. If you want to have subtitles for your video, please embed it in the video using the handbrake app or other apps.
  4. Transcode (convert) the video to a friendly format and manageable file size (< 100 MB) and a standard aspect and resolution like 1280x720 (720p), 1920x1080 (1080p).
  5. A maximum of 100 MB video file size is allowed. You can convert using the Handbrake app.
  6. The maximum length of videos with file size limits: 3 mins
  7. Make sure the video includes the paper title, authors, and a mention of PETRA 2023.
  8. Note that there are free and commercial solutions to help with recordings, such as Screen Recorder or Panopto.

Review Process

Demo committee will review and decide on all submissions based on:


  1. Relevance to PETRA topics
  2. Quality and potential of the IN-PROGRESS research described
  3. Impact and quality of the research in terms of its potential for future contributions.
  4. Novelty / unique nature of research
